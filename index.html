What semantic elements are?

Sematic is the meaning of words and phrases in language. It describes meaning to both the browser and developer. Main semantic tags for larger document outlines are header, nav, aside, section, article, and footer.


Examples of semantic elements: "form", "table", and "article" - Clearly defines its content.
Examples of non-semantic elements: "div"and "span" - Tells nothing about its content.
